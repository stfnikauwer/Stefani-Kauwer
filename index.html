Services

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy

This cognitive behavioral therapy is done by detecting and modifying unhealthy thought patterns, behaviors, and emotions that cause disorders.

Acceptance and Commitment Therapy

Training patients to realize, accept their own feelings and thoughts without judging, and can help patients to be committed to changing their feelings/behavior which is not healthy.

Psychodynamic Therapy

Approach method through in-depth conversations with patients to encourage them to be more open to problems, fears, or dreams.

Art Therapy

Interpret the patient's feelings and mental condition by observing the artwork produced by the patient by freeing him in expressing himself through art, such as painting, coloring, or drawing.

Psychoeducation

An approach method that aims to provide education privately or in groups about certain mental health disorders and how to overcome them.

Interpersonal Therapy

An approach method that aims to modify the patient's behavior and mindset in establishing relationships with others, as well as to overcome mood disorders such as bipolar disorder and so on.
